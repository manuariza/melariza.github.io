The Studio

All conservators at DeMoreto studied at the Higher College of Conservation and Restoration of Cultural Property of Madrid (ESCRBCM) and graduated with a BA in Art History.

DeMoreto Studio performs three different levels of intervention on the artworks:

  • Preventive Conservation: in order to prevent future damage, treatments are carried out within the context or on the surroundings of an item.
  • Conservation: treatments whose main purpose is to extend and maintain as long as possible the condition of the materials of which the object is constituted.
  • Restoration: treatments whose main goal is the restitution or improvement of the readability of the image and the recovery of its unity.

Conservation and restoration procedures are the result of an interdisciplinary work that requires the collaboration of several specialists. At DeMoreto we collaborate with external groups of professionals such as art historians, documentalists, chemists, physicists, archaeologists, architects and photographers.
